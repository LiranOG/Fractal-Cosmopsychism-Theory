## What Is Fractal Cosmopsychism Theory?

Fractal Cosmopsychism Theory (FCT) is a mathematical framework proposing that consciousness is a universal information-processing phenomenon. The theory describes how any complex system — from a single neuron to the cosmic web — can be modeled as an "information bottleneck" that compresses noisy input into useful representations. The central prediction: this compression efficiency is approximately the same across all scales of organized complexity.

## The Core Equation

The theory's primary measurable quantity is **IB efficiency**:

$$\eta_{\text{IB}} = \frac{I(T; Y)}{I(X; T)}$$

This ratio measures how effectively a system ($T$) extracts relevant predictions ($Y$) from its input ($X$). The Universal IB Invariance Conjecture (UIBIC) predicts that $\eta_{\text{IB}}$ is approximately constant ($\sigma < 0.08$) across systems with comparable levels of integrated information.

## The Research Program

We propose a 5-paper progressive proof structure targeting the world's top scientific journals:

**Paper I** (Nature Neuroscience, £280K, $n = 60$): Demonstrate that IB efficiency tracks consciousness level across waking, anesthesia, sleep, and psychedelic states.

**Paper II** (PNAS, £420K, $n = 120$): Show that critical slowing down precedes developmental stage transitions in palliative care patients and meditation retreaters.

**Paper III** (Translational Psychiatry, £180K, $n = 120$): Validate that depression-related rumination oscillates with a measurable person-specific period, detectable via smartphone monitoring.

**Paper IV** (Nature Medicine, £1.8M, $n = 120$): Test whether psilocybin's antidepressant effect is predicted by a brain-derived "presence" biomarker — enabling precision psychedelic medicine.

**Paper V** (PRL/Nature Physics, £95K, 6 datasets): Test the UIBIC across 30 orders of magnitude in physical scale using neural, social, ecological, and cosmological data.

## What We Know and What We Don't

**Already confirmed (retroactive):**
- IB efficiency is approximately constant across visual cortex ($\sigma = 0.055 < 0.08$)
- Critical slowing down precedes depressive episodes
- Post-mortem brains show a gamma power surge before electrical silence
- The IB principle is mathematically equivalent to the Renormalization Group

**Not yet validated:**
- No measurement of the "free will" variable $W(t)$ from EEG data
- Cosmic-scale pilot shows IB efficiency 93× below prediction
- The 15 model parameters are constrained by only 6 independent measurements
- Zero prospective pre-registered tests have been conducted

## Epistemological Integrity

The theory maintains strict intellectual honesty through a 4-tier classification:

- **Tier A:** Directly measured (none yet)
- **Tier B:** Testable formal analog (most formulas)
- **Tier C:** Indirectly testable (cosmic applications)
- **Tier D:** Metaphysical, permanently untestable (foundational axioms)

Every publication must declare which tier each claim belongs to, and every measurement must include an "X-Proxy Declaration" stating that the proxy is not identical to the theoretical construct.
